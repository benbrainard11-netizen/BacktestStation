## Interpretation guide

- **Strong feature**: top decile mean / bottom decile mean ratio ≥ 1.5,
  monotonic or near-monotonic trend, holds across symbols.
- **Weak feature**: ratio < 1.2 or U-shape with no clear direction.
- A feature that's strong pooled but weak per-symbol is suspicious
  (likely catching cross-symbol heteroskedasticity, not per-symbol risk).
- The 2 untested features (iceberg_refills, l2_to_l5_imbalance) need
  book reconstruction — validated in Phase 2 (build_features.py).
